Step by Step

Setup Guide

Follow these steps once and you're done forever.

Chrome Extension

Install on your Desktop

Download Extension ZIP
  1. 1
    Download the extension ZIP above and unzip it to a folder on your computer.
  2. 2
    Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions
  3. 3
    Toggle on Developer mode in the top-right corner.
  4. 4
    Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped extension folder.
  5. 5
    The 📦 Meesho Scan icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar. Click it and sign in with your email and password.
Keep the Meesho Returns page open at supplier.meesho.com/returns while scanning.
Mobile App (PWA)

Install on your Phone

Open this URL on your phone: https://scanserver.techseventeen.com
  1. 1
    Open Chrome on Android and navigate to the URL above.
  2. 2
    Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right.
  3. 3
    Tap "Add to Home screen" and confirm.
  4. 4
    Open the app from your home screen, sign in, and allow camera permission.
  1. 1
    Open Safari on iPhone and navigate to the URL above.
  2. 2
    Tap the Share icon (box with arrow pointing up) at the bottom.
  3. 3
    Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
  4. 4
    Open the app from your home screen, sign in, and allow camera permission.
Use the same email and password on your phone and the extension.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free. There are no subscriptions, no hidden charges, and no limits on scans.

Why isn't the extension on the Chrome Web Store?

The extension is privately distributed and installed via Chrome's built-in "Load unpacked" developer mode — a standard practice for internal tools. It takes less than a minute to install and works identically to a store extension.

Does this work without internet?

The server needs to be reachable. If your server is hosted on a VPS, you need a working internet connection. The mobile app caches itself and works offline for basic use.

What barcode formats are supported?

Code 128, Code 39, EAN-13, EAN-8, QR Code, ITF, UPC-A, UPC-E, and Data Matrix — all common formats used on courier return parcels.

Can multiple people use the same account?

No — one mobile session and one extension session are allowed per account at a time. If you sign in from another device, the previous session is automatically disconnected. Create separate accounts for separate users.

What if the barcode doesn't scan?

Use the manual input field below the camera view on the mobile app, or the manual search input in the extension popup. Type the AWB directly and press Enter.

Is my data safe?

Your password is stored as a bcrypt hash — we never store plain text passwords. Sessions use signed JWT tokens. Scan data (AWB numbers) is stored only on your own server. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What happens if the extension disconnects?

The extension and mobile app both reconnect automatically. If a scan arrives while the extension is offline, it shows as "pending" in the mobile app's scan history.
